6 Degrees Of Separation

This is a project which visualises the social connections of developers in a GitHub repo
and checks if the idea of "Six degrees of separation" applies to developers.

Six degrees of separation - is the idea that all people are six, or fewer, social connections away from each other.
As a result, a chain of "a friend of a friend" statements can be made to connect any two people in a maximum of six steps.

Please enter the address of a GitHub repository into the field below to see a graph of all the connections of its developers.
Example input: nating/cs-exams
